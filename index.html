About Me

Jake Cole is a singer, songwriter, and storyteller from Indiana that now resides Houston, Texas, bringing honest, heart-driven music to every stage. With a passion for crafting songs that capture life’s moments—big and small—Jake blends soulful lyrics, driving melodies, and a love for live performance into a sound that’s uniquely his own. From backroads anthems to intimate acoustic moments, his music invites listeners to feel, remember, and celebrate the moments that make life worth singing about. Jake’s energetic performances and heartfelt songs have earned him a growing following across social media and streaming platforms, and he’s just getting started. Whether he’s writing from his heart in the studio or connecting with fans on stage, Jake Cole is all about music that feels real, personal, and unforgettable.
